Choosing Between Observable and Subject in RxJS The choice between Observable and Subject depends on the requirements of your application: Use Observables when you need a new and independent execution of the stream for each subscriber. This is suitable for scenarios where you want subscribers to receive the entire sequence of events from the beginning. Use Subjects when you need to share the same execution of the stream among multiple subscribers, and when you want more control over pushing new values into the stream. Subjects are ideal for scenarios where you need to maintain state or broadcast events to multiple observers.
